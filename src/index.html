notes

Currently, the above statistics are for concerts only. Data on lectures will hopefully follow shortly.

Composer counts reflect pieces played rather than the raw number of composers on a programme. For example, 3 works by the same composer on a programme will be counted 3 times towards the gender-balance.

Performer counts are done by concert rather than by work. A performer may appear in just one or all works, but they will always only be counted once per concert.

Where ensembles rather than individuals are listed, performer data is omitted unless an accurate personnel listing for that concert can be sourced.

Workshop concerts involving participants of the summer courses are not currently included as the programmes and performers are not yet published.

Data is sourced from the website of the Internationales Musikinstitut Darmstadt, with additional performer data sourced from programmes printed during the summer courses.
